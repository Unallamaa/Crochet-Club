Crochet Club

What is Crochet Club?

Crochet club is a once weekly hour long google meet for crochet enthusiests to share their latest projects and talk while working together. I created this to test my coding skills and develop a community for crocheters of all ages and levels.

Requirements

You will need:

  • A Crochet Hook
  • Something to crochet with (yarn, plarn, etc.)
  • A device that can connect to google meet with camera and microphone capabilities
  • An hour every week

You do not need to attend every week, just come when you can!
